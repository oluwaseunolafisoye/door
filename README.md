# door

door is an AI-powered CV tailoring platform. Upload your CV, paste a job description, and door rewrites your resume to maximise keyword match against the role, generates a matching cover letter, and gives you a full editor to refine everything before exporting as PDF.

## Features

- **CV parsing** — upload PDF, DOCX, or TXT and AI structures it into profile, experience, education, skills, projects, and certifications
- **Resume tailoring** — rewrites bullet points using the CARL framework to hit 90%+ keyword coverage for the target job
- **Cover letter generation** — produces a job-specific cover letter from your CV and the job description
- **Optimization analysis** — keyword match %, requirements covered %, and top matched skills across all applications
- **Live editor** — edit every section of your resume and cover letter with a split-pane PDF preview
- **PDF export** — download your tailored resume and cover letter as print-ready PDFs
- **Dashboard** — bento grid overview of your optimization stats, recent activity, and matched skills
- **History** — full log of every tailored application

## Stack

Next.js · React · Convex · OpenAI · Tailwind CSS · shadcn/ui · React PDF
